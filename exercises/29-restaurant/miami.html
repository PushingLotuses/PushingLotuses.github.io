Yepa's

MIAMI

In the heart of Downtown Miami. Our very first restaurant established in 2023. Where every detail is designed to transport you to a magic World. As you enter Yepa's, you’re greeted by a warm breeze, and it becomes clear that this isn’t just dinner—it’s a waterfront dining experience that beckons you to relax and unwind. The sky shifts from soft orange to deep purple as the sun sets, painting a breathtaking backdrop to your evening as you enjoy your meal or your Lovers Lane Lemonade.

As you sip, you notice the other guests around you, laughing and enjoying themselves, each one caught up in their own moment of joy.

The air is filled with the sound… Of clinking glasses and lively conversation. It’s as if you’ve entered a hidden oasis in the heart of the city. Then, there’s the food—vibrant, creative, and bursting with flavor. Each dish is an invitation to explore, to share, and to savor. You start with a selection of tapas, each one more enticing than the last, and move on to a main course that leaves you wondering how it could get any better. But it does, with a dessert that feels like a sweet dream. By the time the evening ends, you’re already planning your next visit, knowing that no two experiences here are ever the same.




Now we have locations in Miami, Orlando, New York City, and growing.
